# Code of Conduct

AgentPay Casper follows the Casper Agentic Buildathon expectations for fair
play, originality, respectful collaboration, and safe ecosystem development.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Keep feedback focused on the project, code, documentation, and user safety.
- Respect different backgrounds, skill levels, and viewpoints.
- Disclose security concerns responsibly.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, spam, plagiarism, malicious code, or abusive
  behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who
violate these expectations. Serious concerns can also be reported through the
Casper Agentic Buildathon support or community channels listed on DoraHacks.
